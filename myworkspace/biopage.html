My Background

My name is Anthony Guerra, and I am a sophmore at Granada High School. I was born in Fremont, California, until I moved to Livermore where I started school, music, and more activities. I enjoy running for Granada High Cross-Country, and have made it virtually my life. My favorite sports (excluding XC) are NASCAR, Formula 1, and Football. I play two instruments, drums and bass, and I am involved in a youth rock band under the name "Jupiter Dayz". Maybe one day we will be playing the Garden (one can hope).
